Lists of Talismans and Minions

Rings And Tailsmans

  • Intimidation Talisman/Ring/Artifact
    Makes a certain level of mobs not attack you
  • Emerald Ring
    Gives you 1 coin per minute
  • Farming Talisman
    Grants you +10% speed while in farming islands & areas
  • Vaccine Talisman
    Grants you immunity to poison (except Witch poison & Wither effect)
  • Farmer's Orb
    Makes an extra crop regrow every 3 seconds. Can be stacked with the Farmer Aura of the farm suit
  • Night Vision Charm
    Grants permanent Night Vision 1 everywhere
  • Speed Talisman/Ring/Artifact
    Grants a certain amout of speed
  • Feather Talisman/Ring/Artifact
    Grants reduced fall damage
  • Piggy Bank (Normal/Cracked/Broken)
    Reduces amount of coins lost upon death. You can repair it as it breaks
  • Potion Affinity Talisman/Ring/Artifact
    Extends the duration of any potion by a certain amount
  • Haste Ring
    Grants 15 seconds of Haste 1 when mining a block
  • Experience Artifact
    Grants +25% more Experience Orbs
  • Talisman of Coins
    Ocasionally spawns 1-5 coins on the ground in public islands
  • Magnetic Talisman
    Increases item pickup range by 3x
  • Day/Night Crystal
    They look the same and cause +5 Attack and Defence during the day or night
  • Gravity Talisman
    Grants +1 to +10 Attack and Defence depending how close you are to an island's spawn
  • Fire Talisman
    Grants immunity to fire
  • Lava Talisman
    Grants immunity to lava
  • Wood Affinity Talisman
    Grants +10% speed in the Forest, Graveyard and Wilderness
  • Healing Talisman/Ring
    Increases healing speed by a cretain amount
  • Bait Ring
    Grants a 5% chance for fish to not consume bait when fished up
  • Sea Creature Talisman/Ring/Artifact
    Reduces a certain amount of damage from sea creatures
  • Titanium Talisman/Ring/Artifact/Relic
    Increases mining speed by a certain amount

Minion Types

  2. Coal Minion
